USE OF COOKIES Cookies are small text identifiers that our site can send to uniquely identify your browser. When you visit our web site, we may send a cookie to your computer to store on its hard drive. Cookies enable our systems to recognize your browser and can improve your use of our site by allowing us to save your status and preferences for return visits and to customize information that would be most relevant for you. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies, but you may be able to change the settings to refuse cookies or to be alerted when cookies are being sent. Although disabling cookies will not interfere with your ability to interact with most of our site, you will need to accept cookies in order to log in and make purchases from our site.
